About the exam

Exam facts

  • Passing score: 700 (on a scale to 1000).
  • Role: analytics engineer — you design, create, and manage analytical assets (semantic models, lakehouses, warehouses) and partner with architects, analysts, and admins.
  • Languages you must know: SQL (T-SQL), KQL (Kusto Query Language), and DAX (Data Analysis Expressions).
  • Question styles: multiple-choice, multi-select, drag-and-drop/ordering, and scenario/case studies. Most questions target GA features; commonly used preview features may appear.
  • Expect to choose the right tool for a scenario as often as to recall a fact — reason about tradeoffs.
